Who We Are

Waterproof Jewellery
That Never Fades

Founded by Rohini Zaware and Prashant Jadhav, Trinora Jewels was created with a vision to bring long-lasting shine into everyday fashion.

The brand is emerging as a modern anti-tarnish jewellery label designed for today's confident women. Built with passion and dedication, Trinora focuses on creating stylish, waterproof, and skin-friendly jewellery that complements both daily wear and special occasions.

With a growing customer base across India, Trinora is steadily building trust by delivering premium-quality jewellery designed for modern lifestyles.
