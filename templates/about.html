Who We Are

Crafting Stories
in Gold & Silver

Trinora was born in 2009 in the Pink City of Jaipur — India's jewellery capital — with a single vision: to create jewellery that transcends trends and becomes a cherished part of your story.

Our founder, Meera Singhania, grew up watching her grandmother wear the same pair of gold earrings every day — not because they were expensive, but because they carried memories. That childhood observation became the guiding philosophy of Trinora.

Today, Trinora employs over 50 skilled artisans, many from families with generations of jewellery-making heritage. We blend time-honoured techniques with modern design sensibilities.

Our Mission

To create fine jewellery that is beautiful, meaningful, and accessible — without compromising on craftsmanship, ethics, or quality. We want every woman to own a piece that tells her story.

Our Vision

To become India's most loved fine jewellery brand by keeping alive the ancient art of handcrafted jewellery while building a sustainable, ethical business that empowers artisans.
